About Us

Welcome to Chattogram Board Students' SSC Ranking website!

We aim to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform where users can explore rankings, search by name or roll number, filter by school, and access historical data. Our goal is to streamline the process of obtaining SSC examination results and rankings, making it quick and efficient for everyone involved.

On our website, you can:

  • Explore Rankings: View the detailed rankings of students across various groups, including Science, Commerce, and Arts.
  • Search by Name or Roll: Use our search functionality to find individual students by their name or roll number.
  • Filter by School: Easily filter and view results from specific schools to compare performance.
  • Access Historical Data: Look up results from previous years to see how students and schools have performed over time.
